Tribute to Stephen Hawking

Early Life

Stephen William Hawking was born on January 8, 1942, in Oxford, England. Despite facing challenges due to ALS, his passion for science emerged at an early age.

Achievements

Hawking made groundbreaking contributions to theoretical physics and cosmology. His work on black holes, the nature of time, and the theory of everything reshaped our understanding of the universe.

Scientific Contributions

Through his work on singularity theorems and Hawking radiation, he transformed our understanding of black holes and the fundamental laws of physics.
